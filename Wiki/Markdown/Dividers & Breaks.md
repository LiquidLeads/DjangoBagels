# Dividers & Breaks

Line breaks use the HTML `break` tag and, like most elements, must be inserted with a blank line on top and bottom of the break.



## Line Breaks

### Syntax: A Single Line Break
```markdown
...content.

<br />

#### New Section

Content.
```

### Output
> ...content.
> 
> <br />
> 
> #### New Section
> 
> Content.



<br /><br />

### Syntax: A Double Line Break
```markdown
...content.

<br /><br />

#### New Section

Content.
```

### Output
> ...content.
> 
> <br /><br />
> 
> #### New Section
> 
> Content.



<br /><br />

### Syntax: A Divider + Line Break
```markdown
...content.

<br />

---

#### New Section

Content.
```

### Output
> ...content.
> 
> <br />
> 
> ---
> 
> #### New Section
> 
> Content.
